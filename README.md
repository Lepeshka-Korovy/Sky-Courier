# ☁️ Sky Courier

**Sky Courier** is an atmospheric sandbox game about a balloon delivery man. 
You travel between floating islands, collect clouds, complete orders, and gradually learn what happens beyond the clouds.

---

## 🎮 What is the game about

- You are a courier. Your job is to deliver cargo and collect clouds in jars.
- There is fuel, cargo capacity, upgrades, and various islands.
- The world hides a secret: Empyrean is a white city above, which does not notice anyone below.
- The player decides whether to climb higher to see the truth.

---

## 🎨 Style

- Low Poly, soft colors, without overload.
- An atmosphere of freedom and tranquility that gradually changes.
